  • Now the smart building can include various sub-applications such as:
    • Smart Parking System for reserving a parking spot for people
    • Emergency Evacuation System for finding safe exits during emergency situations
    • Smart Guest Guidance System to automate the guest check-in process
    • Personal Tracking System for keeping a record of each individual in the building and record their preferences
